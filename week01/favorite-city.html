MY FAVORITE CITY | NAIROBI

Nairobi is my favorite city because it is vibrant, diverse, and full of opportunities. As the capital city of Kenya, Nairobi is known for its modern skyline, rich culture, and friendly people. One of the things I love most is that it is the only capital city in the world with a national park, where visitors can see wildlife just a short distance from the city center. Nairobi is also a center for business, education, and innovation, making it a great place to live, work, and learn. No matter where I travel, Nairobi will always hold a special place in my heart because it represents home, growth, and countless unforgettable memories.
